Our History
Dr. Sampat's Sainik School Preparatory Institute (SSPI), Pune was founded in 2017 with a vision to guide young aspirants towards careers in the Armed Forces. Led by Dr. Sampat Wadekar—an ex-faculty of NDA—the institute blends academic excellence with military-style discipline. At SSPI, students are trained rigorously to crack entrance exams for Sainik Schools, RMS, RIMC and the prestigious National Defence Academy (NDA).
Our Mission
To be one of India’s leading defence preparatory institutions and a strong feeder to NDA, Sainik School, RMS, and RIMC.
Our Vision
To transform ordinary students into responsible, confident, and capable youth ready to serve the nation.
